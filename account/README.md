# Account and Registration

> This section walks you through account registration and asset management on the platform.

## Content Overview

- **[Bridge Assets](bridge-assets.md)**: Learn how to bridge your assets from the Bittensor chain to the Bittensor EVM chain
- **[Account Registration](registration.md)**: Step-by-step instructions for completing account registration
- **[Deposits and Withdrawals](deposit-withdraw.md)**: How to Deposit and Withdraw assets

## Preparation

Before getting started, please make sure that:

1. Your Bittensor wallet and EVM wallet are properly installed and configured
2. Your wallets contain sufficient assets to complete the required operations
3. You are familiar with basic blockchain transaction workflows
4. You are prepared to cover network gas fees

## Important Notes

- Safeguard your wallet private keys and mnemonic phrases at all times
- Always review transaction details carefully before confirming
- Monitor network congestion and adjust gas fees accordingly
- Keep your wallet secure and stay alert to phishing websites

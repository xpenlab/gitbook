## Before You Start

Make sure you have completed the following:

- ✅ **EVM Wallet** (e.g. MetaMask)
- ✅ **Bittensor Wallet**
- ✅ TAO or ALPHA tokens bridged to the Bittensor EVM chain
- ✅ Sufficient gas fees in your EVM wallet

⚠️ **Important**: You must bridge your assets from the Bittensor chain to your EVM account before using Taolend.

👉 **For detailed bridging instructions**, please refer to [Bridge Assets Guide](account/bridge-assets.md)

## Lender Quick Guide

### Step 1: Connect Wallet and Register

1. **Access the Platform**
   - Open the Taolend website
   - Click **Connect Wallet** in the top-right corner

<div align="left">
  <img src="images/connect_wallet.png" alt="Connect EVM Wallet"/>
</div>

2. **Connect Your EVM Wallet**
   - Choose MetaMask (or another EVM wallet)
   - Confirm the connection in your wallet

3. **Complete Registration**
   - Go to the **Profile** page and click **Register**
   - Sign an message to verify your identity
   - Confirm the signature in your wallet
   - **Confirm the registration transaction**
   - Wait for confirmation ‚Äî registration is complete

<div align="left">
  <img src="images/register.png" alt="Register" width="400"/>
</div>

### Step 2: Deposit TAO

<div align="left">
  <img src="images/navigation.png" alt="Navigation"/>
</div>

1. **Go to the Profile Page**
   - Click **Deposit**
   - Select **Free Tao** or **0 - Root**

<div align="left">
  <img src="images/deposit.png" alt="Deposit" width="400"/>
</div>

2. **Enter Amount**
   - Enter the TAO Amount you want to deposit
   - Review the details

3. **Complete Deposit**
   - Click **Deposit**
   - Confirm the transaction in your wallet
   - Wait for confirmation (about 10‚Äì30 seconds)

### Step 3: Create Loan Offer

<div align="left">
  <img src="images/lend_offers.png" alt="Lend Offers" width="600"/>
</div>

1. **Go to the Lend Page**
   - Click **+ New Offer**

2. **Configure Offer Parameters**
   - **Subnet**: The Subnet of ALPHA collateral you accept
   - **Amount**: Maximum TAO you are willing to lend
   - **Daily APR**: Daily interest rate (0.01% ‚Äì 1%)
   - **Maximum Acceptable Price**: Upper price limit for ALPHA collateral
   - **Expire in**: Validity period of the offer

<div align="left">
  <img src="images/new_offer.png" alt="New Offer" width="600"/>
</div>

3. **Sign and List**
   - Click **List**
   - Sign with your wallet (offline signature)
   - The offer is instantly visible on the Market

### Step 4: Manage Loans and Offers

1. **Wait for Borrowers**
   - Your offer appears on the Market
   - Once accepted, TAO is automatically lent out
   - You will be notified

2. **View Active Loans**
   - Go to **Lend ‚Üí Active**
   - Track loan status and interest accrual in real time

3. **Manage Offers**
   - Create multiple offers with different parameters
   - Cancel individual or all offers at any time
